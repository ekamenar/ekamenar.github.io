We are pleased to announce the launch of our project, (<a href="https://ekamenar.github.io/projects/HRZZ_2024_ASoRo/">Advanced Soft Robots: Data-Driven Development, Modeling and Control (UIP-2025-02-1541)</a>), funded by the Croatian Science Foundation.

---

### Project objectives

- **O1. Clinical requirements:** Investigate patient needs and clinical requirements influencing the development of soft biomechatronic rehabilitation devices through collaboration with medical experts.  
- **O2. sEMG feature extraction:** Establish data processing methodologies for extracting surface electromyography (sEMG) features enabling grip force forecasting.  
- **O3. Koopman-based forecasting:** Develop data-driven Koopman-based methodologies for grip force forecasting.  
- **O4. Soft device prerequisites:** Investigate prerequisites for pneumatically driven soft biomechatronic devices in rehabilitation robotics, including kinematic analyses, material selection, characterization and modeling, and device design.  
- **O5. Data-driven control:** Formulate data-driven controllers for soft rehabilitation robots using Koopman operator theory to enable adaptive and personalized rehabilitation strategies.  
- **O6. Prototype validation:** Develop and experimentally validate a proof-of-concept soft robotic rehabilitation prototype.

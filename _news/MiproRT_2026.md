The **Call for Papers** for *MIPRO Robotics 2026* is now open!

---

MIPRO Robotics is part of the MIPRO 2026 convention and brings together researchers and practitioners to present and discuss recent advances in robotics, intelligent systems, automation, and related technologies.

The conference provides an excellent platform for sharing new research results, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaboration between academia and industry.

We encourage researchers, engineers, and students to submit their latest work and contribute to the scientific discussion at this year’s event.

For conference details, topics of interest, and submission guidelines, please visit:

- <a href="https://mipro.hr/MIPRORobotics2026/ELink.aspx" target="_blank">MIPRO Robotics 2026 – Conference Information</a>
- <a href="https://mipro.hr/LinkClick.aspx?fileticket=9EiStS3N6Y0%3d&tabid=110&mid=461&language=en-US&forcedownload=true" target="_blank">Call for Papers (PDF)</a>

We look forward to your submissions and to an exciting MIPRO Robotics 2026.

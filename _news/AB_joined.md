**Andrej Borić** joined our research group as a **Postdoctoral Researcher**. Welcome, Andrej!

---

As part of the Croatian Science Foundation (HRZZ) project **Advanced Soft Robots: Data-Driven Development, Modeling and Control**, Andrej will contribute to research focused on the mechanical design and development of soft robotic systems for applications in robotic rehabilitation.

His work will particularly support the development of 3D models, 3D printing of soft robotic structures, materials engineering, and the experimental characterization of materials and prototypes. With his background in advanced polymer materials, CAD modeling, and experimental research, Andrej will strengthen our group’s efforts to design and fabricate innovative soft robotic actuators and rehabilitation-oriented robotic devices.

We are excited to have Andrej on board and look forward to working together!

I am pleased to share that I will be giving an **invited talk** at the **RoboSoft 2026 Workshop on the Role of Control in Soft Robotics**, held in conjunction with IEEE RoboSoft 2026.

---

The workshop brings together leading researchers to discuss fundamental and emerging questions related to modeling, estimation, and control of soft robotic systems. It will feature a distinguished lineup of invited speakers addressing both theoretical advances and practical challenges in the field.

I look forward to contributing to the discussion on data-driven modeling and control approaches for soft robotic systems, and to engaging with colleagues working at the intersection of soft mechanics, robotics, and control theory.

The **call for posters is now open**. We warmly encourage students and researchers to submit their work and participate in what promises to be an exciting and forward-looking workshop.
